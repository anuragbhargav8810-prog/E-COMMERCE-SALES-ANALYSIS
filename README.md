# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** is an interactive data visualization project designed to analyze and monitor the performance of an online retail business. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights that help stakeholders understand trends, customer behavior, and product performance.

It provides a clear view of important business metrics such as **total sales, quantity sold, orders, category performance, brand performance, and platform comparison**. With interactive filters for **brand, category, product, and month**, users can easily explore and analyze different segments of the business.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The main business goals of this dashboard are:

* 📊 Monitor **overall sales performance** of the e-commerce business.
* 🛍 Identify **top-performing product categories**.
* 🏷 Discover **best-selling brands** based on sales and quantity.
* 📅 Analyze **monthly sales trends and seasonal fluctuations**.
* 🛒 Compare **sales performance across e-commerce platforms**.
* ⭐ Evaluate **customer ratings and satisfaction levels**.
* 📈 Support **data-driven decision making** for business growth.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights from the Dashboard

* 💰 **Total Sales:** 302M indicating strong overall business performance.
* 📦 **Total Quantity Sold:** 30K products sold across all categories.
* 🧾 **Total Orders:** 10K customer orders processed.
* 📊 **Top Categories:** Electronics and Fashion generate the highest sales.
* 🏆 **Top Brands:** Xiaomi, Samsung, JBL, Nike, and HP contribute significantly to revenue.
* 🌐 **Platform Performance:** Amazon contributes around **66% of total sales**, while Flipkart contributes **34%**.
* 📉 **Monthly Trend:** Sales fluctuate throughout the year with peaks in **mid-year and festive months**.
